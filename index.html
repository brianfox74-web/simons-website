Why Tuckley?

We believe in slow making. Each terrarium takes time — sourcing the right glass, choosing plants by hand, and arranging them until the composition feels just right.

Whether you're looking for a statement piece for your home, a heartfelt gift, or a bespoke commission, we bring the same level of care and craft to every single build.

No two terrariums are ever the same. That's not a limitation — it's the whole point.
