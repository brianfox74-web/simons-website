How It Works

From idea to living artwork

01

Enquire

Reach out via our contact form or email. Tell us what you're looking for, your budget, and any ideas you have.

02

Design

We discuss your vision and propose a design — vessel shape, plant selection, and overall composition.

03

Build

Your terrarium is hand-built with care. We layer substrates, plant by hand, and let the piece settle before it leaves us.

We pack your terrarium securely and ship it to you, along with full care instructions so it thrives for years to come.
